R Shiny Project: Coronavarius Visualization App

I created this R Shiny App in course BIOSTAT 203B Introduction to Data Science . Used pacakge including tidyverse, dplyr, ggplot2, shiny to tidy and visualize the data from the COVID-19 Data Repository by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University. Source code of the app was uploaded in my GitHub Repository.
[ Go to the App]

P.S. This R Shiny App was created at the beginning of the pandemic (Feb 2020). At That time, the maximum country-wise confirmed case number was less than five thousands. It was really shocking when I had to adjust the slider range from 1~5000 to 1~90,000,000 before republishing the App for my website. I really hope this pandemic ends soon. ü§û
