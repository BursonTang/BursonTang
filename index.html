Hello.

I am currently a fourth-year Coastal Engineering Ph.D. student in the Coastal Flood lab at UCLA, advised by Prof. Timu W. Gallien . My research focuses on compound flood modeling at urban coastal region, considering sea level rise, marine and hydrologic forcing interactions and urban infrastructure. Before this, I obtained my B.S. degree from College of Hydrology and Water Resources at Hohai University in 2018, and was selected to participate UCLA-Hohai exchange program.

I am also a self-motivated data science and programing learner. Playing with model source code, analysis and visualzation of hydrodyanmic data and modeling results are not enough to satisfy my interest.I have been taking relevant courses from UCLA (BIO)STATS departments and MOOC website and completed many interesting projects. I would love to try a data scientist or SDE position after graduation!
